Tritanium Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2/19/2025

Introduction

Tritanium is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

Data Collection and Storage

  • Local Storage: All personal data is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain any external servers for personal data storage.
  • ESI Authorization: We access the EVE Online ESI API only with your explicit authorization, which is required for features related to your game account.
  • User Account: This app does not require (nor does it have) an app-specific account. Any mention of an account refers to your EVE Online account. Additionally, you can still use certain app features without logging into your EVE account.
  • Game Data: The app collects and processes EVE Online game data through the official ESI API, strictly limited to the permissions you grant.

Data Usage

  • App Functionality: Your game data is used solely to provide app features and functionality.
  • No Analytics: We do not collect analytics or tracking data about your app usage.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not share your personal data with any third parties.

User Control

  • Authorization Control: You can revoke ESI authorization at any time through the EVE Online website.
  • Data Deletion: You can delete all local data by removing the app or using the in-app reset function.
  • Cache Management: The app provides options to manage cached data and clear it when needed.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect or solicit any information from anyone under the age of 13. If you are under 13, please do not attempt to use the app or send any information about yourself to us.

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have the right to request information regarding the disclosure of your personal information to third parties for their direct marketing purposes. However, as stated above, we do not share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights, including:

  • The right to access, update or delete your personal information
  • The right of rectification
  • The right to object
  • The right of restriction
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to withdraw consent

Tritanium 隐私政策

最后更新：2025年2月19日

简介

Tritanium 致力于保护您的隐私并确保您的个人数据安全。本隐私政策说明了我们在您使用我们的应用程序时如何收集、使用和保护您的信息。

数据收集与存储

  • 本地存储：所有个人数据仅存储在您的设备本地，我们不维护任何用于个人数据存储的外部服务器。
  • ESI授权：我们仅在获得您的明确授权后访问EVE Online ESI API，这是与您的游戏账号有关的功能所必需的。
  • 用户账号：该应用并不需要（也不存在）应用账号，该应用中所提到的账号指的是EVE Online的账号，而且即使您不登陆EVE账号，也可以使用部分应用功能。
  • 游戏数据：应用程序通过官方ESI API收集和处理EVE Online游戏数据，严格限于您授予的权限范围内。

数据使用

  • 应用功能：您的游戏数据仅用于提供应用功能，不会用于其他目的。
  • 无分析数据：我们不收集关于您的应用使用情况的分析或跟踪数据。
  • 不共享第三方：我们不会将您的个人数据分享给任何第三方。

用户控制

  • 授权控制：您可以随时通过EVE Online网站撤销ESI授权。
  • 数据删除：您可以通过删除应用或使用应用内的重置功能来删除所有本地数据。
  • 缓存管理：应用程序提供选项来管理缓存数据，并在需要时清除它。

儿童隐私

我们的应用不会故意收集或征求13岁以下任何人的信息。如果您未满13岁，请不要尝试使用该应用或向我们发送任何关于您的信息。

加州隐私权

如果您是加利福尼亚州居民，您有权要求了解有关我们向第三方披露您的个人信息用于直接营销目的的信息。但如上所述，我们不会与第三方共享您的个人信息用于营销目的。

隐私政策的变更

我们可能会不时更新我们的隐私政策。我们会通过在本页面上发布新的隐私政策并更新本隐私政策顶部的"最后更新"日期来通知您任何更改。

您在GDPR下的权利

如果您是欧洲经济区（EEA）的居民，您享有某些数据保护权利，包括：

  • 访问、更新或删除您的个人信息的权利
  • 更正的权利
  • 反对的权利
  • 限制处理的权利
  • 数据可携带性的权利
  • 撤回同意的权利
